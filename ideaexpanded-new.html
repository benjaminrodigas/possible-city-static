Miss Mac is a pop-up art program that unites high school students and artists in the community to create an exhibition to raise funds for maintaining and creating programs in their city.

Waitlists, lottery programs, and registration fees are becoming the norm for organizations looking to suppleâ€‹ment where the school systems are falling short in regards to the arts. This demonstrates, not only a need, but also a demand for the arts in these communities.

MIss Mac is a one month program that acts as an open studio for high school students that will be hosted by various local artists. There will be a gallery show at the end of the month highlighting artwork created during the program by the students and participating artists.

A portion of the proceeds from the shows will be donated to organizations already committed to bringing arts to the community. Eventually Miss Mac will offer small grants to participating artists, as well, to help continue the art out-reach.
